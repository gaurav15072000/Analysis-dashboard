# Analysis-dashboard

Total Leads: The dashboard tracks a total of 32 leads.

Lead Sources:
Majority of the leads come from inbound sources like website visits (43.8%), followed by exhibitions (18.8%) and inbound calls (12.5%).

Weighted Forecast: The forecasted output based on the leads is 336.29 metric tons.

Lead Status:
93.8% of leads are currently open, while only 6.3% are closed.

Lead Stages:
The largest percentage of leads, 56.3%, are in Stage 4 (advanced stage).
21.9% are in Stage 2, while 15.6% are in Stage 3.

Lead Region:
Leads are spread across various regions, with significant contributions from Asia and Europe.

Lead Type:
The majority of leads (87.5%) are categorized as warm leads, indicating potential but not immediate conversion.
